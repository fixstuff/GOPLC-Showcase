Part V is the industrial-protocol reference. GoPLC ships with first-class client and server implementations of nineteen protocols, from Modbus TCP and RTU through EtherNet/IP, Siemens S7, OPC UA, MQTT, Omron FINS, DNP3, IEC 60870-5-104, BACnet/IP, SNMP, Allen-Bradley DF1, Sparkplug B, and SEL Fast Message, and into the data-layer and specialty protocols (InfluxDB, G-code streaming, protocol analyzer with store-and-forward, NMEA GPS, DMX/sACN/MIDI/OSC entertainment, and a grab-bag of utility protocols including KNX, M-Bus, ZPL, and BACnet-over-MS/TP). Each chapter is self-contained: architecture overview, configuration, every ST function with parameter tables, and worked examples. Keep this part bookmarked — it is the part of the manual you will return to most.
